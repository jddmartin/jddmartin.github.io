Phys 360/460 Experiment: Radio-frequency electronics and frequency stabilization

The basic goal of this experiment is to illustrate the usage of various modular radio frequency components (splitters, mixers etc...) by stabilizing the frequency of a voltage controlled oscillator using a cavity resonance.
